Frequently Asked Questions

These are some of the commonly asked questions by our users, along with detailed answers to provide you with the information you need to understand and engage with the Eye of God initiative.

  • What is Eye of God?

    Eye of God is a pioneering initiative that leverages the power of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) to address the critical issue of missing individuals in India. Our mission is to reunite missing persons with their families by harnessing cutting-edge technology and a compassionate team of experts.

  • How does Eye of God work?

    Our system uses AI and ML algorithms to analyze data, including images and videos, to aid in the identification and location of missing individuals. By processing and cross-referencing information from various sources, we provide valuable insights to law enforcement agencies and families involved in the search

  • How does Eye of God prioritize privacy and data security?

    We take data privacy and security seriously. We employ advanced encryption techniques to protect sensitive information and ensure that user data remains secure. We adhere to stringent data protection standards and regulations.

  • Are there any success stories or notable achievements of Eye of God?

    Yes, we have successfully assisted in locating and reuniting missing individuals with their families. We have received recognition and praise for our efforts, and you can find testimonials and success stories on our website.

  • What are the future plans and goals of Eye of God?

    Our future plans involve expanding our technological capabilities, reaching more communities, and collaborating with law enforcement agencies to make our system even more effective in reuniting missing individuals with their families.
